What is BeatBoost ?

BeatBoost is a meditation and relaxation app that designed to take you some moment to relax and breathe. BeatBoost mission is to help you find your inner peace and improve your mental health.

How can we help you ?

BeatBoost helps you to relax your body and mind with meditation music, nature scenery with sounds, an AI voice asistant to talk to and breathing exercises to help you cope with your stress and anxiety. BeatBoost can help you to let go of the busy mind, and relax your body.
